The Story: From Idea to Collection

See how a creation is born, ignites, and grows on Zora.

01 • Research

Find the Tone & Identity

The creator gathers sketches—visuals, music, or writing—then decides on the edition's theme: limited or open edition.

Pro tip: use premium mockups and a 10–15 second cinematic teaser to spark curiosity.
02 • Release

One-Click Mint

Upload assets, set the price, edition size, start/end time, and split addresses for collaborators. Hit Publish—it's ready to go live.

Secret: schedule a simultaneous minting session (a drop party) to create an initial wave.
03 • Community

Collectors Become Narrators

Every mint adds to the story. Collectors showcase the work, review it, and invite friends. The impact grows exponentially.

Keep it going: airdrop a bonus edition to early holders to maintain momentum.

Ready to Write Your Story on Zora?

Start by creating your first edition. Upload your work, set the parameters, and then share the mint link with your community.

Create First Edition See the 5-Minute Guide
  1. Prepare your asset (image/audio/video) + a short description.
  2. Decide the type: limited edition or open edition.
  3. Set the price, quantity, start time, and split addresses.
  4. Publish. Share the mint link on social media.
  5. Celebrate with your collectors; plan a bonus for early holders.
